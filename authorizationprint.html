Middle Kingdom Martial Activities Card
This card is your authorization card to participate in the SCA activites on the field in
the  Midrealm.   It  must  be  presented  to  the  List  Officials  along   with  proof  of
membership (i.e. - a blue membership card or Pale label with signed waiver) at  SCA
events  to  register  for participation  and  you  may  be equested  to  show  it  to  any
member of the masrchallate at any time.  This cardis the property of the office of the
Midrealm Earl Mashal.  The bearer of  this card  acknowledges  that they  have  read
the rules  of  the list  and  conventions of  combat for the SCA  and Middle Kingdom
